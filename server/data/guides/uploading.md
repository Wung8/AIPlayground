## Log In

Create an account or log in using the **Login** button in the top navigation bar.

## Go to an Environment

Click **Environments** in the nav bar and select an environment.

## Upload Your Bot

1. On the play page, open the **My Bots** tab in the left sidebar
2. Click an empty **Upload Bot** slot
3. Select your `.py` file

Your bot will appear in the slot once uploaded. Click it to assign it to a player, then press **Reset** to start the game.
